<topic>
	<title> Installing scanner driver  </title>
	<text> 
Procedure


Insert the CD containing the scanner driver into the CDROM.
In Windows Explorer, browse and select the drive containing the device. 
Double click on setup.exe or the appropriate executable file.
Follow the on screen instructions.

Instruction Click the Play icon to view the video demonstration on how to install the scanner driver
	</text>

<topic>
	<title> Scanning an image or document </title>
	<text> 

	Procedure
		Launch the scanner application or utility on your computer.
		Place a photo or document in the scanner.
		Click Scan on the scanner application.
		Follow the prompts to save the scanned image on your computer.
	</text>
